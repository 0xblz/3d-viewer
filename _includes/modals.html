3D Viewer

A browser-based 3D model viewer for engineers, architects, designers, and developers. Open and inspect 3D models directly in your browser — nothing is uploaded or stored.

How it works

  1. Tap Open to select a 3D model file
  2. The model loads instantly in the viewer
  3. Use the controls to rotate, zoom, pan, and inspect

Supported formats

DAE, OBJ, GLB, STL, FBX, IFC, 3DS, WRL, and PLY

Controls

  • Rotate: Drag or swipe
  • Zoom: Pinch or use +/- buttons
  • Pan: Two fingers or arrow buttons
